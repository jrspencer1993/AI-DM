Cleric

“Faith is not weakness. It is the will to command miracles.”
Clerics are chosen vessels of the divine, bridging mortals and gods. Their prayers are power given form—healing, warding death, and smiting foes with divine fury. Not all clerics serve peaceful gods; some embody war, judgment, death, or prophecy.

Role: Divine Caster, Support, Healer, Protector

HIT POINTS
- Hit Dice: d8 per Cleric level
- Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier

PROFICIENCIES
- Armor: Light armor
- Weapons: Simple weapons
- Tools: None

CORE RULES (HYBRID)
- Base Attack Bonus Progression: HALF
- Primary Stats: Wisdom, Charisma
- Skill Points per Level: 4 + Intelligence modifier
- Class Skills: Arcana, History, Insight, Medicine, Religion, Perception

============================================================
CLASS FEATURES
============================================================

LEVEL 1: Spellcasting, Divine Domain

FEATURE: Spellcasting
As a Cleric, you have access to divine magic. Your spells come from your connection to your deity and are granted through prayer and meditation. You prepare spells from the Cleric spell list. The number of spells you can prepare is based on your Cleric level as shown in the Cleric Spellcasting Table.

Cantrips Known: See Cleric Spellcasting Table.
- When you gain a Cleric level, you can replace one known cantrip with another from the Cleric spell list.

Spell Slots: See Cleric Spellcasting Table.
- You regain all expended spell slots when you finish a Rest.

Prepared Spells of Level 1+: See Cleric Spellcasting Table.
- At 1st level, you begin with 4 prepared Cleric spells of 1st level.
- When you gain Cleric levels, your prepared spells maximum increases as shown on the table.
- Whenever you finish a Rest, you can change your list of prepared spells (must be spells you have slots for).

Spellcasting Ability: Wisdom
- Spell Save DC = 8 + spell level + your Wisdom modifier
- Spell Attack Modifier = base attack bonus + spell level + your Wisdom modifier

Spellcasting Focus
- You can use a holy symbol as a spellcasting focus for your Cleric spells.

FEATURE: Divine Domain
At 1st level, choose a Divine Domain that represents your god’s influence and grants you specific abilities.
- Domain Spells are always prepared and don’t count against your prepared spells maximum.
- Each domain grants Domain Spells and a Domain Feature at 1st level.
- Additional domain features are gained at 3rd, 6th, and 9th levels.

LEVEL 2: Channel Divinity

FEATURE: Channel Divinity
You can call upon your god to invoke a Channel Divinity. This power can be used to turn undead or to fuel a specific domain feature.

Turn Undead
As an action, present your holy symbol. All undead creatures within 30 feet must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the undead are turned for 1 minute or until they take damage. Turned creatures must spend their turns moving away from you by the safest route.

Domain-Specific Channel Divinity
At 2nd level, depending on your domain, you gain a unique Channel Divinity option.

LEVEL 3: Divine Domain Feature
You gain an additional feature granted by your chosen Divine Domain.

LEVEL 4: (No base class feature)

LEVEL 5: Sacred Writ

FEATURE: Sacred Writ
Your understanding of holy scripture allows you to draw on divine truths in critical moments.
- As a reaction, when you or a creature within 30 feet makes a saving throw against a spell or magical effect, you can speak a verse of your faith’s scripture to grant divine clarity.
  The target may reroll the saving throw and must use the new result.
- Uses per day: Wisdom modifier times per day (minimum once).
- You regain all uses after a Rest.

LEVEL 6: Divine Domain Feature
You gain an additional feature granted by your chosen Divine Domain.

LEVEL 7: Sanctified Blows

FEATURE: Sanctified Blows
Divine power infuses you in battle. Choose one option:
- Divine Strike: Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack roll, the target takes +1d8 Necrotic or Radiant damage (your choice).
- Potent Spellcasting: Add your Wisdom modifier to the damage you deal with any Cleric cantrip.
If you would gain either option from an older Cleric subclass, use only the option you choose for this feature.

LEVEL 8: (No base class feature)

LEVEL 9: Divine Domain Feature
You gain an additional feature granted by your chosen Divine Domain.

LEVEL 10: Divine Intervention

FEATURE: Divine Intervention
As a Magic action, choose any Cleric spell of level 5 or lower that doesn’t require a Reaction to cast. As part of the same action, you cast that spell without expending a spell slot or needing Material components.
- Once per Long Rest.

LEVEL 11: (No base class feature)
LEVEL 12: (No base class feature)
LEVEL 13: (No base class feature)

LEVEL 14: Improved Sanctified Blows

FEATURE: Improved Sanctified Blows
The option you chose for Sanctified Blows grows more powerful.
- Divine Strike: extra damage increases to 2d8.

LEVEL 15: Living Conduit

FEATURE: Living Conduit
Once per day when you are reduced to 0 HP, your divine patron may intervene—delaying death for 1 round.
- You remain conscious and may act as normal.
- You fall unconscious at the end of your next turn if not healed.

LEVEL 16: (No base class feature)
LEVEL 17: (No base class feature)
LEVEL 18: (No base class feature)
LEVEL 19: (No base class feature)

LEVEL 20: Greater Divine Intervention

FEATURE: Greater Divine Intervention
When you use your Divine Intervention feature, you can choose Wish when you select a spell. If you do so, you can’t use Divine Intervention again until you finish 2d4 Long Rests.
