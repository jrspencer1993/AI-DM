Class: Wizard
Role: Master of arcane magic and knowledge

Hit Dice: d4
Hit Points at Level 1: 4 + Constitution modifier

Proficiencies:
Armor: None
Weapons: Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows
Tools: None
Saving Throws: Intelligence, Wisdom
Base Attack Bonus: 1/2 BAB
Skill Points per Level: 4 + Intelligence modifier
Skills: Arcana, History, Insight, Medicine, Religion

========================================
Class Features
========================================

Level 1:
Spellcasting (Sp)
- Spellcasting Ability: Intelligence
- Spell Save DC = 8 + spell level + Intelligence modifier
- Spell Attack Modifier = Base Attack Bonus + spell level + Intelligence modifier
- Cantrips Known: 3
- Spellbook: 6 first-level wizard spells
- Prepared Spells: Intelligence modifier + Wizard level (minimum 1)
- Ritual Casting: May cast ritual spells from spellbook without preparing
- Spellcasting Focus: Arcane focus

Familiar (Sp)
- Gain a familiar via 24-hour ritual (100 gp)
- Familiar HP = Wizard level + Intelligence modifier
- Familiar Intelligence = Intelligence modifier (minimum 6)
- Telepathic communication within 100 feet
- Familiar can deliver touch spells at level 6

Ritual Adept (Sp)
- May cast any ritual spell from spellbook without preparing it

Level 3:
Magic School Specialization (Sp)
Choose one:
- General: Learn one extra spell per spell level (1st–5th)
- Abjuration: Increase AC bonus from abjuration spells by +1
- Conjuration: Reaction teleport 15 ft (Int mod uses/day)
- Divination: Reaction d4 fate twist (Int mod uses/day)
- Enchantment: Reaction daze attacker (Int mod uses/day)
- Evocation: Reaction ranged force attack (scales by level)
- Illusion: Cast an illusion cantrip when casting illusion spells
- Necromancy: Familiar becomes undead
- Transmutation: Gain temporary HP equal to Int mod

Level 6:
School Mastery (Sp)
- General: Gain another spell of each level
- Abjuration: Arcane Ward (2 × Wizard level + Int mod HP)
- Conjuration: Minor Conjuration (nonmagical object)
- Divination: Third Eye (Truesight 60 ft)
- Enchantment: Split Enchantment
- Evocation: Add Int mod to damage
- Illusion: Alter long-duration illusions
- Necromancy: Necrotic resistance, HP max can’t be reduced
- Transmutation: Permanent object transmutation

Level 18:
Spell Mastery (Sp)
- Choose one 1st-level and one 2nd-level spell
- Cast them at will at lowest level

Level 20:
Arcane Mastery (Sp)
- Choose a school of magic
- Spells of that school are cast as if one slot level higher
- May concentrate on two spells of different schools
