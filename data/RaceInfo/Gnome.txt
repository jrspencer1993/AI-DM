Gnome

Speed: 25
Size: Small
Darkvision: 60

Ability Bonuses:
* INT +2

* Magic (Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma saves)

Subraces:
* Rock Gnome: CON +1, Artificer's Lore, Tinker
* Forest Gnome: DEX +1, Natural Illusionist, Speak with Small Beasts
* Deep Gnome: DEX +1, Superior Darkvision (120 ft), Stone Camouflage
