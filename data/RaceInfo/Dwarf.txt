Dwarf

Speed: 25
Size: Medium
Darkvision: 60

Ability Bonuses:
* CON +2

Proficiencies:
* Battleaxe, Handaxe, Light Hammer, Warhammer (weapons)
* Smith's Tools or Brewer's Supplies or Mason's Tools (tool)

Resistances:
* Poison damage
* Poisoned condition

Traits:
* Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.
* Dwarven Resilience: You have resistance to poison damage and saving throws against poison.
* Stonecunning: Whenever you make an Intelligence (History) check related to the origin of stonework, you are considered proficient and add double your proficiency bonus.
* Dwarven Combat Training: You have proficiency with the battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, and warhammer.

Languages: Common, Dwarvish

Subraces:
* Hill Dwarf: WIS +1, Dwarven Toughness (+1 HP per level)
* Mountain Dwarf: STR +2, Light and Medium armor proficiency
